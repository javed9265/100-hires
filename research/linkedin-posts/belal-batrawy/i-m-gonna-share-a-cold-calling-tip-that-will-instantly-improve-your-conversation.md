## Short Source Excerpt

## Research Notes

- This post is useful because it focuses on exact cold call language.
- Belal argues that words like "help" and "struggle" can make the seller sound assumptive or patronizing.
- The better move is to describe a common situation and ask how the prospect handles it.

Use this source in the call opener section: avoid diagnosing the buyer too early and ask about their current process instead.

# [Playbook] Cold Email: Analyzing 85M+ cold emails to find what’s working in 2025
*Expert:* Jason Bay
*YouTube:* https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yjOG_QOJgII
*Published:* 2025-07-24

## Key Takeaways
- Cold email still works, but the old volume-first model is weaker because inboxes are crowded and spam filters are more aggressive.
- Smaller lists with stronger intent signals are more defensible than broad persona lists.
- The best outbound teams target moments of change, not just job titles.
- Plain text, short copy, and one clear call to action reduce friction.

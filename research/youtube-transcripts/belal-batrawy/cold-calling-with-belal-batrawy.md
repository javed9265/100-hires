## Why This Source Was Collected

Belal Batrawy broadens the project beyond email into cold calling. A real cold outreach pipeline for B2B SaaS should include phone strategy when the buyer and ACV justify it.

## Key Ideas

- Cold calls need directness, relevance, and control of the opening seconds.
- The purpose of a call is often to create a useful conversation, not pitch everything.
- Sellers need to sound like they understand the account context quickly.
- Calls can support email by creating attention and urgency.

## Useful Playbook Implications

- Pair phone touches with email instead of treating them as separate channels.
- Write call openers around relevance and permission.
- Track conversations and outcomes, not only dial count.
- Use calls to test whether email messaging is clear.

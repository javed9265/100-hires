## Key Takeaways
- Cold calls need directness, relevance, and control of the opening seconds.
- The purpose of a call is often to create a useful conversation, not pitch everything.
- Sellers need to sound like they understand the account context quickly.
- Calls can support email by creating attention and urgency.

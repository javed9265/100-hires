## Why This Source Was Collected

Jesse Ouellette is included because deliverability and data quality decide whether cold email reaches the buyer at all. This source adds technical operating depth to the research set.

## Key Ideas

- Email waterfall design helps teams find and validate better addresses before sending.
- Deliverability problems can make good copy look bad.
- Cold email systems need monitoring, not one-time setup.
- Data quality and inbox health should be checked before judging campaign performance.

## Useful Playbook Implications

- Add email verification and waterfall logic before campaign launch.
- Separate deliverability metrics from copy metrics.
- Monitor bounce rate, inbox placement, domain health, and positive replies.
- Treat data operations as part of the outreach pipeline.

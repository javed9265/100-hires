## Key Takeaways
- Generic persona-based outbound is weaker than problem-led outbound.
- Sales teams need sharper hypotheses about why the buyer should care.
- Good outbound messaging should sound like it understands the buyer's world.
- The best playbooks are built from patterns that reps can repeat, not one-off clever lines.

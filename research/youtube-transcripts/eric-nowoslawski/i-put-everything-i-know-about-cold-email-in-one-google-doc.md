## Key Takeaways
- Cold outreach performance depends on infrastructure, list quality, and message quality working together.
- List building is not just data collection; it is hypothesis building about who has the problem now.
- Good campaigns need a clear offer before automation matters.
- AI is useful when it improves research or relevance, but weak when it creates generic copy.
